About this Course

Organizations worldwide are using process optimization to reduce waste, increase efficiency, and deliver better customer experiences. Healthcare administrators use process mapping to shorten patient wait times. Logistics companies use it to optimize delivery routes. Financial institutions use it to streamline compliance workflows.

Process improvement is a powerful methodology that uses data-driven techniques to analyze and enhance business processes. Using tools like process mapping and Lean principles, professionals can identify bottlenecks, eliminate redundancies, and create measurable business impact.

This isn't theoretical. You will analyze a real company's process (anonymized data from actual BlendedSkill partners), identify $100,000+ in savings, and present a professional improvement proposal. Your capstone is a portfolio piece that proves you can deliver ROI on day one—not a simulation.

Learning Objectives

  • Learn the fundamental techniques and tools required to analyze and improve a business process
  • Gain experience with common process mapping methodologies and notation standards
  • Enhance processes through data analysis to identify improvement opportunities
  • Leverage Lean and Six Sigma concepts to achieve efficient results
  • Build confidence to lead your own process improvement project
  • Develop skills to calculate ROI and present business cases to stakeholders

Topics Covered
